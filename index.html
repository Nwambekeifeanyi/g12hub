The Visionary

Chukwu Joshua Chukwuebuka

Founder, G12 Institute

| 09035466348

Chukwu Joshua Chukwuebuka is a leadership strategist, transformational speaker, and purpose-driven mentor dedicated to raising a generation of individuals who lead with integrity, excellence, and impact. He is the founder of the G12 Institute, a leadership and human development organization committed to equipping people with the knowledge, character, and practical skills needed to thrive in every area of life.

With a deep passion for leadership, identity, purpose, and personal transformation, Joshua has devoted his life to helping individuals discover their God-given potential, overcome limitations, and become influential leaders in their families, communities, careers, and nations.

Through the G12 Institute, he champions a holistic approach to human development built on five core pillars: Kingdom Development, Leadership Development, Financial Development, Capacity Building, and Personal Development. His teaching emphasizes that true success is achieved when the spirit, soul, and body are intentionally developed and aligned with purpose.

Joshua is known for his practical, thought-provoking, and inspiring approach to teaching. His vision is to build a community of purpose-driven leaders who will influence society with wisdom, competence, and godly values, creating lasting transformation across generations.

He believes that leadership is not merely a position but a responsibility to serve, empower, and inspire others to become the best versions of themselves.
